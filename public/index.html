Frequently Asked Questions

Is EpiSave a medical device?

EpiSave is an assistive tool designed to help detect seizures, but it is not a replacement for medical care or supervision. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Which Android smartwatches are compatible?

EpiSave works with most Wear OS smartwatches and select other Android-compatible wearables. Check our compatibility list for specific models.

How accurate is the seizure detection?

While no detection system is perfect, EpiSave uses advanced algorithms to achieve high accuracy. The app continuously improves through machine learning and user feedback.

How does the alert system work?

When a potential seizure is detected, EpiSave sends notifications to designated emergency contacts via SMS and app notifications, including the user's GPS location when available.
