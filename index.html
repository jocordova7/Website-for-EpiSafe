Why Choose EpiSave?

With over 65 million people affected by epilepsy worldwide, accessible monitoring solutions are more important than ever. EpiSave transforms any Android smartwatch into a powerful seizure detection tool that's:

  • Accessible - Works with the Android smartwatch you already own
  • Reliable - Uses advanced motion sensors to detect seizure-like activity
  • Supportive - Keeps your caregivers informed with instant alerts

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EpiSave a replacement for medical care?

No, EpiSave is designed as an assistive tool to help detect seizure-like activity. It is not a medical device and should not replace professional medical care or monitoring.

Which Android smartwatches are compatible?

EpiSave works with most Android smartwatches running Wear OS 2.0 or higher with built-in accelerometer sensors.

How accurate is seizure detection?

While EpiSave uses advanced algorithms to detect movement patterns common in seizures, it may not detect all seizures and may occasionally issue false alerts. Always consult your healthcare provider about appropriate monitoring options.
